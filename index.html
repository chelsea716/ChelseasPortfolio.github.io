About Me

My name is Chelsea Etheridge and I am a passionate motion graphics artist and animator dedicated to bringing ideas to life through visually stunning animations. I enjoy learning and am still devewloping my skills through trial and error. I am proficient in industry standard software such as Adobe After Effects, Maya, and Photoshop.

Projects

Project 1: Dragon! A Videogame Background.

Background For video game designers/developers. Mockup idea of a background/foreground for a video game. Test and animate an original design.

Problem New idea or concept for a scene in a video game.

Process Get a few references for inspiration. Sketch a few ideas on Procreate. Refine sketches. Add color and details. Animate the idea. I approached this with the type of art style in video games that I enjoy.

Results I enjoyed how the design came out and learned a bunch along the way. This could have turned out better if I used a software such as Maya so that I could have made the environment 3D which I was not sure of how to do at the time.

Project 2: Helping Inform People

Background For news and sports channels. Design and animated lower thirds. To showcase information as it is needed on TV.

Problem Introduce a matching design for different scenarios whereas needed.

Process Take in what details I want to add to the design. For this one in particular, I used the logo for inspiration. I added in the team's colors. My goal was to make it simple but eye catching and different.

Results I enjoy how the design came out. This could have turned out better if I added in a more complex animation for how the lower thirds swoops in.

Project 3: Reviving a Small Business

Background For a company that needed a new logo.

Problem A dying company that needed to keep up with modern times.

Process Interview the owner for what he liked and envisioned for his company. Sketch out a few designs and get feedback. Finalize the one the client liked.

Results I had a bunch of ideas which showed in all of the sketches that I designed. It could have come out better if I added in the year the company was founded to show the years of experience.
